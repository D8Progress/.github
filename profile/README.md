# D8 Progress Organization

> **Deliber8 Progress (D8 Progress)** - Gamified goal tracking designed for neurodivergent brains

## ğŸ¯ Mission

Traditional productivity apps fail neurodivergent users because they assume habits become automatic and punish missed days instead of celebrating wins. **D8 Progress is different.**

We're building a goal tracking platform specifically designed for ADHD, autistic, and other neurodivergent brains that provides:

- **Ongoing dopamine rewards** - XP, levels, and achievements for every action
- **Forgiveness mechanics** - Streak shields and grace periods, no shame spirals  
- **Flexible structure** - Multiple objective types that match how you actually work
- **Context-aware organization** - Goals scoped to life areas that matter to you

## ğŸš€ Core Features

### Goal & Task Management
- **Hierarchical Organization**: Categories > Goals > Objectives > Tasks
- **Four Objective Types**: Simple, Checklist, Milestone, and Habit tracking
- **Weekly Planning**: Allocate tasks by week with smart rollover
- **Eisenhower Matrix**: Priority-based task management
- **Quick Search**: Find anything instantly with `Ctrl+K`

### Gamification System
- **XP & Leveling**: Earn experience for completed tasks
- **Achievement System**: Unlock badges for milestones and streaks
- **Streak Tracking**: Build momentum with consecutive completions
- **Streak Shields**: Protect your progress on difficult days
- **Visual Celebrations**: Confetti and satisfying feedback

### Party System (Planned)
- **Character Creation**: Role-playing elements for different life areas
- **Gold & Shop**: Unlock cosmetics and rewards with in-app currency
- **Team Goals**: Collaborative objectives with friends/family
